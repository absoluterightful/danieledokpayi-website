About

Daniel Edokpayi is an independent artist creating music shaped by faith, African sound, and real life. His music carries a message of hope for people walking through struggle, guilt, loneliness, and uncertainty. Through Afrogospel, Afro R&B, and AfroSoul, Daniel’s goal is simple: to remind people that God has not left them.
