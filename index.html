About

Faith. Sound. Story.

Daniel Edokpayi is a Nigerian-born independent artist creating music shaped by faith, African sound, and real life. His songs carry messages of hope for people walking through loneliness, guilt, uncertainty, and difficult seasons.

Through Afrogospel, Afro R&B, and AfroSoul, Daniel’s mission is simple: to make honest music that reminds people that God has not left them.
