Veil Of Hope

A debut album in progress, built around faith, restoration, worship, and the hope God gives in uncertain seasons.

The first chapter is Assurance, the lead single arriving July 31, 2026. The full album is being shaped as a blend of Worship, Afrobeats, Afro R&B, and AfroGospel.
