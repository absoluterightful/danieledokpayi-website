Never Leave Me

“Never Leave Me” is a song of pure relief and gratitude for God’s constant presence. It captures the exact moment the tears start falling and the world feels like it’s crashing down, only to realize that Jesus is right there holding your hand and leading you back to His grace.

It’s an easy-to-understand, heartfelt prayer about a Savior who never says no, never walks away, and always opens a door when you feel completely lost.

It’s simple, personal praise for the One who picks us up every single time life gets rough.
